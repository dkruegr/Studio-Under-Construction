Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions that need answers? Here are a couple common ones we get asked by viewers

What makes DKreative Studio different from other agencies?
We follow the ADDIE Model to deliver successful projects. We start by analysing your needs, then design a blueprint with wireframes and brand elements. Next, we develop the project, implement it, and finally evaluate to ensure long-term results.
Will we be able to communicate directly with designers?
Yes, you’ll have a clear line of communication with our designers, with our team facilitating every step to deliver the best results.
How fast are projects completed?
Project timelines can be influenced by factors such as project scope, complexity, design requirements, technical considerations, and client collaboration. Throughout the process, we maintain clear communication and careful management, striving to deliver each project on the mutually agreed timelines.
What E-commerce platforms do you support?
We work particularly with WordPress and Shopify, but we also support other platforms such as Webflow and Framer. If you’re using a different platform, just let us know your requirements and we’ll find the best way to help.
